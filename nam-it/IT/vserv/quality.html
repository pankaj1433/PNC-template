An unrelenting committment to industry leading quality standards

Continuous Quality Improvement

Valley Services Electronics’ commitment to total customer satisfaction is built on a foundation of the tightly controlled processes of our ISO 9001 and 13485 registered Quality Management System. From the first point of contact, through the entire product life cycle, VSE provides capabilities and expertise that differentiate us from other EMS solution providers. This stems from the ability of our processes to identify and prevent challenges in the earliest stages of the manufacturing cycle.

The result is a fully compliant First Article, delivered on-time, guaranteed. Post-delivery, VSE’s customer satisfaction strategy is equally effective, by focusing on ongoing communication and fast, flexible responses to customer needs.

Product Design

VSE can provide a full service product or subsystem design or assist your team with product development incorporating manufacturability from the start.

Design Team Partner

VSE Engineering will work with the customer design team to identify potential manufacturability and testability issues before the prototype design is finalized.

  • Develop manufacturing process strategy and detail process map
  • Develop manufacturing test strategy and test fixture requirements
  • Perform component availability analysis and offer alternatives to achieve minimum lead time and longest product life.
  • Maintain a product cost model and identify opportunities to reduce product cost.
Product Design Resource

VSE Engineering can be your product design resource. VSE can create a product or subsystem design that satisfies your requirements.

  • Experienced staff to perform electrical and mechanical design.
  • Provide complete manufacturing documentation.
  • Manage NRTL approvals.
