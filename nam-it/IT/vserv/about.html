Provide expert end-to-end electronics services and turn-key solutions to meet our customer’s business objectives.

VSE was established in 1981. Like so many Silicon Valley success stories, VSE was started in a garage. Hard work, customer focus and continuous improvement established us as a leader in the contract manufacturing industry.

With headquarters located in the heart of Silicon Valley, we are in the center of technological innovation. As a full service electronics manufacturing company, we serve a wide range of technology companies worldwide.

We have succeeded by consistently providing flexible turnkey solutions to our customers. If you are looking for an experienced electronics assembly partner that will produce high quality products, deliver on time at a competitive cost, VSE should be your choice.

Excellence In End-To-End Electronics Services

From design to manufacturing, we provide turn-key solutions for clients worldwide. With a commitment to exceeding quality standards, our work exceeds expectations.
