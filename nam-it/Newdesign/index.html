Willowbrook Farm subdivision, situated in the city Novi, is part of Oakland County in the state of Michigan, is located on North of 10 Mile Rd /East of Meadowbrook Rd. Novi, judged as one of the fastest growing city in Michigan, ranked 32nd among the best places to live in the US, is areputable business hub, making it a well-rounded community. With access to I-275 / I-96 less than two miles away, WFSD is part of the reputed Novi school district, consistently ranked among the top school districts in the US. Willowbrook Farm is a sprawling habitat, carved out of a wooded landscape, laced by a swirling stream of Bishop creek, unseemingly camouflaged from the din, soaking in tranquil, abundant in flora, enlivens the lingering freshness. We take pride in welcoming you to the community!
